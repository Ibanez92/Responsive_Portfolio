About me

Hello, welcome to my portfolio. My name is Javier Ibanez. I was born in Paterson, New Jersey, on April 1, 1992. My parents both come from the South American country Peru. I have a wife and we've been together for 9 years. We have a 4-year-old son that we adore and his name is Leonardo. We live in Davenport, FL, about 20 mins from Walt Disney World.


My passion is in improving myself every day. I use to be a supervisor for a flooring company before the pandemic and now I'm aspiring to be a web developer. I enrolled myself in a 3-month coding boot camp, at the University of Central Florida and it was tough at first, but I got through it. I learned so much and my teachers were awesome, but I know I have a long journey ahead of me. I have gained a lot of respect for anyone who codes. It's not the easiest to grasp, but when you do, it's an awesome feeling of self-accomplishment. I can't wait to continue my journey! Thanks for taking the time to read about me and I welcome anyone who wants to connect with me.
